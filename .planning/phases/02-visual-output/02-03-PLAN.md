    build_deck(data: FinancialData, metrics: MetricsOutput) -> Presentation:
    - Returns a python-pptx Presentation with 11 slides in the specified section order
    - Every slide has a navy header bar (_add_header) and navy footer bar (_add_footer)
    - Chart slides embed a matplotlib PNG BytesIO in the content area
    - Slide count: 11 (within 12-15 range) — see slide structure below

    Slide structure (11 slides total):
    1. Title slide — company name (large, centered), "Financial Performance Review", period covered (e.g. "FY2020 – FY2024"), generation date; NO navy header (full-slide navy background instead, white text)
    2. Executive Summary — header "Executive Summary"; 3-5 bullet lines with pre-computed metric values as placeholder narrative (e.g. "FY2024 Revenue: $XXX B | Net Margin: XX.X%", "Revenue Growth CAGR (FY2020–FY2024): XX.X%", etc.)
    3. P&L — Revenue Trends — header "P&L | Revenue Trends"; bar_chart of Revenue by year
    4. P&L — Margin Analysis — header "P&L | Margin Analysis"; line_chart of gross_margin, ebitda_margin, net_margin
    5. P&L — Earnings Bridge — header "P&L | Earnings Bridge"; waterfall_chart Revenue→GrossProfit→EBITDA→NetIncome
    6. Balance Sheet — Asset Composition — header "Balance Sheet | Asset Composition"; bar_chart of Total Assets by year
    7. Balance Sheet — Working Capital — header "Balance Sheet | Working Capital"; line_chart of working_capital and current_ratio (dual or two separate series)
    8. Balance Sheet — Leverage — header "Balance Sheet | Leverage"; bar_chart of Total Debt by year
    9. Cash Flow — Operating vs Free — header "Cash Flow | OCF vs FCF"; bar_chart side-by-side OCF and FCF (use grouped bar or two bar_chart images — simplest: bar_chart of FCF values)
    10. Cash Flow — Trend — header "Cash Flow | Trend"; line_chart of free_cash_flow over years
    11. KPI Scorecard — header "KPI Scorecard"; full-width kpi_scorecard image

    Title slide design (slide 1 only — different from all others):
    - Full-slide navy rectangle (0, 0, SLIDE_W, SLIDE_H) as background
    - Company name: white, Calibri, 36pt, centered vertically and horizontally
    - Subtitle "Financial Performance Review": white, Calibri, 20pt
    - Period line: white, Calibri, 14pt (e.g. "FY2020 – FY2024")
    - Date line: white, Calibri, 12pt (date.today().strftime('%B %d, %Y'))
    - Still gets footer applied in second pass

    - _embed_chart(slide, buf: io.BytesIO) -> None: add_picture at CONTENT_LEFT, CONTENT_TOP, CONTENT_W, CONTENT_H
    - _add_text_block(slide, text: str, top=CONTENT_TOP, left=CONTENT_LEFT, width=CONTENT_W, height=CONTENT_H, size=SZ_BODY) -> None: add textbox with word_wrap
